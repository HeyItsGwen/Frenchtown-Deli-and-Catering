Welcome

All of us at Frenchtown Catering would like to welcome you to our website. We are a premium catering company, serving in and around St. Charles County in Missouri. We provide appetizer and dinner options to weddings, parties and other special events. We are located in Frenchtown of the historic district of St. Charles.

About Us

Frenchtown Catering Company is a premium caterer located in Frenchtown of the Historic District of St. Charles, Missouri.

Established in 1994, we offer premium selections to make your special occasion or event, a night to remember and cherish. Whether you’re seeking simple box lunches for a corporate event, or planning your wedding; we take the time to ensure you receive close attention and high quality service before, during, and after the event.

In contrast to typical catering companies, we offer completely customizable menus and options. Of course, we offer the traditional favorites, but sometimes you may want more. At Frenchtown, we don’t stand behind a “set-in-stone” menu; we tailor our options to best meet your needs.

Have your very own idea or a family recipe you want reproduced to a large group? We work with you to ensure we create your special dish/serving as if you fixed it at home. If you can think it, we can create it!
